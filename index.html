Our Donate Plan

Our intentions are to provide Emergency Aid to those in need and help the
flood victims of Bangladesh with food shelter and education for a child.

Food for family

We are providing food, water, medicine and also aid to help rebuild flood affected families in the following areas of Sylhet: Osmaninagar , Bishwanath, Zokiganj.

Shelter for family

Emergency shelters or transitional shelters are a common issue in the aftermath of disasters. Your donation can help a family to rebuild their shelter.

Education for a child

Donate for education and ensure the academic development of the flood effected child
